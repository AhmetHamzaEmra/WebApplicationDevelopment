Noah's Ark Petting Zoo

Located in Soutlnvick, Massachusetts

Come visit Noah's Ark Petting Zoo. Ride ponies, have a sleepover in the hay loft or wander freely among the animals in our petting zoo. The petting zoo includes rabbits, ducks, pigs, llamas, sheep, pygmy goats, miniature donkeys and miniature horses.

Our barn provides a large heated party room with picnic tables, grill, refrigerator and restrooms, We can provide you with souvenir hats and pictures and will be glad to suggest a variety of games,
