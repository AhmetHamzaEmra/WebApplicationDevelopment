Yasin Öztekin (Turkish pronunciation: [ˈjasin ˈøztecin]; born 19 March 1987) is a Turkish
professional football player who plays as a winger for Galatasaray in the Süper Lig, and Turkey
national team. He has previously played in Borussia Dortmund's academy and youth teams.
