Alex Nicolao Telles (born 15 December 1992) is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a
left-back for Portuguese club Porto. He played most of his professional career in Brazil, most notably for
Grêmio. Telles is an offensive full-back and is described as a skilled, quick and playmaking defender.
Telles is highly rated in Brazil and was rewarded with a place in the 2013 Brasileirão Team of the Year.
He also has an Italian passport.
