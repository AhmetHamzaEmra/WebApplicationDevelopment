Description:Amethyst is a violet variety of quartz often used in jewelry. The name comes
from the ancient Greek ἀ a- ("not") and μέθυστος méthystos ("intoxicated"), a reference to the
belief that the stone protected its owner from drunkenness. The ancient Greeks wore amethyst and
made drinking vessels decorated with it in the belief that it would prevent intoxication. It is one
of several forms of quartz. Amethyst is a semiprecious stone and is the traditional birthstone
for February.
